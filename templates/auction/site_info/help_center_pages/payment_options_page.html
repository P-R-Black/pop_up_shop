What are the Payment Options

We currently accept PayPal, Venmo, Visa, Mastercard, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, DAI and USDC as forms of payment.

Does The Pop Up Accept Cryptocurrency as Payment

At this time "The Pop Up" does accept payment in the DAI and USDC stable coins.

Payment Processing

PayPal, Venmo, credit card, debit card, Apple Pay and Google Pay payments are processed through Stripe.

DAI and USDC payments are processed through BitPay.
